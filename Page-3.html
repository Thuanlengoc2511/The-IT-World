The robot has become very popular these days. It appears anywhere, but mostly in Korea. The first time, the robot is just a machine with limited actions, but nowadays, it can do many things. The robot has become a robot service in a restaurant. This is a massive impact on the world. A robot can become like a human than before and have a human shape. Moreover, the robot is most applied in construction and fabrication. 
Today, scientists are trying their best to upgrade the robot to a new version. What they can do now is researching and keeping create more robots for other industries. Furthermore, they can improve old version robots with new functions to save money instead of creating more robots. Decrease electric usage will make robot active longer and do more work. These days, robots have been applied in many fields of industries. It helps human work faster and decreases the failure rate. Robots can work in the service industry, such as a coffee shop or restaurant; they can take the order and bring drinks and foods for the customers. Moreover, the robot can work in manufacturing enterprises. 
Robots have a massive role in this industry because it creates products faster and better than humans. The robot can help bring the heavy object safer and quicker than humans. Today, robots have many benefits in the world. In the next three years, robot technology might be applied to public security. Robots can do dangerous work for the human. If they are used to public security, the crime and accident rate will decrease. Because robots can detect threats faster and more accurately than human eyes, it can immediately prevent the threat. Moreover, robots can be used in healthcare. The robot can work for a long time and do not feel tired, so taking care of patients is suitable for robots. Instead of checking patients' conditions often by humans, the robot can now check on their requirements and evaluate the need for further appointments.
 Automatic recognition of suspicious activities helps the robot detect and recognize suspicious activities and prevent them. This system is already available for the security camera. If we can combine it with the robot, we will create a new type of robot. Today, there are some robot machine can help the doctor to make a surgery. I think that we can make a healthcare robot base on it and robot service.

What is the likely impact?

Robot development has been one of the most critical industries in the world. It has a lot of impact on economic society. The robot has many potentials to develop. They can replace humans in many industries, such as healthcare, building. This will also bring up both advantages and disadvantages. The benefit is works faster and better than before because the robot can work for a long time without a break. However, the robot will replace humans and lead to a lack of jobs or a redundant workforce; many people can be unemployed. Employees are affected the most. They can lose their job because of the robot. However, the robot also creates more jobs for people. Robots have both good and bad effects; some tasks can not replace by the robot.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

What does it do?

 AI is the most exciting and discussed technology in the current decade to mimic human intelligence. AI is developing personal, social, and business aspects every day. AI can be used to control the system automatically and make a specific plan to follow. Moreover, AI can play some entertainment such as chess, music, painting, and it can be used in automobile, logistics, Healthcare, stock-trading, robotics, transport, education like industries. In recent years, AI can read and write an essay; it can also see the world and collect data from it to learn. Furthermore, AI can hear and understand what the human said and reply by voice or action. AI can act like a human, such as smell, move, play games, debate, and especially understand emotions. In the next three years, AI can improve transportation. This may take more time than three years to perfect the AI for the vehicle, but it can be used in no time. Also, for manufacturing, AI can work alongside humans to make it quicker and safer. Still, to make this 100% automatic will need more time to complete. In Healthcare, with AI technology, diseases are faster and more accurately diagnosed, drug discovery is sped up. There are more that AI can do, such as education and the media. Natural Language Generation(NLG), Machine Learning, are the technologies that allow AI to become possible.

What is the likely impact?

AI technology has a massive potential that can allow humans to focus on significant work and indulge in more leisure activities. AI can improve the performances of our workplaces and can increase the output humans can do. When AI takes over repetitive or risky tasks, it frees up the human workforce to do the work they are better at, including creativity and compassion. The worker and customer will be the one who is affected the most because AI can work better than humans, so many people will lose their jobs because of AI. However, AI can create more employment for humans base on their data and information. Also, humans can work alongside AI can increase the efficiency of work. AI helps people do the most challenging part, and they only need to focus on their primary mission to finish it as soon as possible. This will improve the performance of working.

How will this affect you?

In daily life, AI is one of the crucial parts of life. It makes life more comfortable to live. They have massive data and information in their memories. Even though we are using AI very often, but we do not recognize it. Open your phone with your face is an AI technology; it records your face and saves it to unlock your phone. Moreover, Netflix uses AI technology to track your style and recommend the film for you. Also, on many websites, they are using AI chatbot to communicate with customers. It will contact will the staff if it has a complicated question. AI is essential to me. I use it a lot in my daily life. So I believe that my friends and my family have the same feeling as me. AI helps our everyday life more comfortable and convenient. Without it, life will be more backward than before.
